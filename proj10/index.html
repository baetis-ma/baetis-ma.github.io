LiPo Battery Tool

This is an esp-idf project with the purpose of outputting a LiPo battery tester and charger. My daughter and I have recently begun flying some drones and find ourselves in posession various 1S, 2S and 3S cells. They were all very inexpensive and/or used and seem to vary a bit in power storage - we would like to check out our batteries to find which are best and build a charger with more capabilities than the ones we have.

Plans are to have a standalone ESP and hardware assembly with a 1inch OLED display, a 3 ohm power load, 0-2A programmable current source and a 4 channel ADC. Assembly control, monitoring and data analysis via webpage.

Design Goals

  • Monitor Cell Voltages of LiPo Battery stack.
  • In Charge Mode : charge each battery to 4.2 Volts at preset current (0-2A) while monitoring charge.
  • In Discharge Mode : monitor discharge of battery into a fixed 3 ohm load while integrating charge to determine battery capacity.
  • In Discharge to 3.8 Volt Mode : get battery to 3.8 Volts by discharging or charging prior to period of inactivity.
  • Using PCF8591
